Matcha Movement Website

Web RCKT was super excited when we got the opportunity to work with Matcha Movement to help build their online community. We pushed ourselves to create a clean, elegant website, that matched Matcha Movement's goals of advocating a healthy, stylish ingredient and culture.

Creating a Community

One of the things Matcha Movement really wanted was to be able to connect with their customers and other matcha lovers from all over the world. We helped create a user base for their site so shoppers could log in and express their thoughts and opinions on everything matcha.

Shop in Style

Creating an elegant online store is crucial to the user experience here at RCKT Web Design. We designed a store for Matcha Movement that highlights the greatest resource they have at their disposal- a foundational base element with matcha. All aspects of the store emphasize the quality of the product and elevate matcha to an artistic level.
